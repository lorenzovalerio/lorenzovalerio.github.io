Lorenzo Valerio, PhD Senior Researcher

I’m a permanent senior researcher at the Ubiquitous Internet group of IIT-CNR in Italy. I design and develop machine learning and distributed learning algorithms for resource-constrained devices, mainly targeting the Pervasive Edge IoT domains. In the past, I worked on cellular traffic offloading solutions based on Reinforcement Learning and autonomic data dissemination algorithms for opportunistic networks.
